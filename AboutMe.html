About Me

Who I Am

I am an instructional designer and eLearning developer based in Utah. With ten years of educational experience, I have fostered a passion for creating meaningful learning experiences. My philosophy is to create equitable, engaging, and creative solutions that help learners and organizations reach their goals. By keeping a learner-centric approach, I create user-friendly and aesthetic eLearning that users enjoy.

Who I (Really) Am

I love exploring new places and learning new things. In my spare time, you might find me drawing, playing board games, tinkering with technology, or playing with my cat. When I can escape the cold Utah winters, you will find me roller skating or hiking the beautiful mountains!
